Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online booking system on our website, by calling us at (123) 456-7890, or by visiting our salon in person. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for weekends and special occasions.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. We understand emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes.

Do you offer bridal packages?

Yes! We offer comprehensive bridal packages that include hair styling, makeup, manicure, pedicure, and spa treatments. We also provide trial sessions before your big day. Contact us for a personalized bridal consultation and package pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, cash, and digital payment methods including Apple Pay and Google Pay. We also offer gift cards that make perfect presents for your loved ones.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have complimentary parking available for our clients. Our parking lot is located directly behind the salon. Street parking is also available nearby. We offer validated parking for appointments over 2 hours.

Do you use cruelty-free products?

Absolutely! We are committed to using cruelty-free and environmentally conscious products. We carry a selection of organic and vegan beauty products. Please let us know if you have any specific product preferences or allergies.

Our Working Hours

We're here to make you look and feel your best. Visit us during our regular business hours or book an appointment online for your convenience. Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are recommended to ensure availability.
