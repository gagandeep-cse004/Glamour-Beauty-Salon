FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to enjoy benefits until the end of your billing period.

Do unused services roll over?

Unfortunately, unused services do not roll over to the next month. We encourage you to use all your included services each month to maximize your membership value.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. The prices shown include everything. Additional services beyond your plan will be charged at the discounted member rate.

Can I share my membership?

Memberships are individual and non-transferable. However, Luxury members receive 2 guest passes per month to share with friends or family.

Is there a trial period?

Yes, we offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, you can cancel within the first week for a full refund.
